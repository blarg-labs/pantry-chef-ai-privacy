Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Introduction

Pantry Chef AI ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Camera Access: We request access to your device camera solely to photograph your fridge or pantry for ingredient detection. Photos are processed by our AI system to identify ingredients and are not stored permanently on our servers.

Local Data: Your preferences, saved recipes, shopping lists, favorites, and macro goals are stored locally on your device using secure storage. This data is not transmitted to external servers unless required for AI processing.

Subscription Information: If you subscribe to Premium, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment card details.

How We Use Your Information

  • Process photos to identify ingredients using AI
  • Generate personalized recipe suggestions
  • Store your preferences and favorites locally
  • Manage your subscription status
  • Improve our AI ingredient detection accuracy

Data Storage and Security

All personal data (favorites, shopping lists, settings) is stored locally on your device. Photos sent for AI analysis are processed in real-time and are not retained after processing. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information during transmission.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Billing: Handles premium subscription payments
  • AI Processing: Photos are sent to our secure servers for ingredient identification and recipe generation. Images are processed in real-time and not stored.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all local app data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling
  • Revoke camera permissions at any time through device settings
  • Cancel your subscription through Google Play
  • Request information about data processing by contacting us

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
